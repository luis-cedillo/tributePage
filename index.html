Marcus Aurelius

The Last Good Emperor of Rome

Emperor of Rome: 161 - 180


Timeline of Marcus Aurelius:

  • 121 AD - Aurelius is born in Rome
  • 140 AD - He becomes Consul of Rome
  • 145 AD - Aurelius marries his cousin, Faustina, daughter of Emperor Antoninus Pius.
  • 161 AD - Antoninus Pius dies. The senate desires Aurelius to become the new emperor. Aurelius decides he wants to co-rule with his adoptive brother, Verus. War breaks out with Parthians.
  • 168 AD - The Germans invade across the Danube. Aurelius begins to author his personal journal which later becomes known as "Meditations".
  • 169 AD - Verus dies. Marcus becomes sole emperor.
  • 175 AD - Marcus Aurelius becomes ill.
  • 180 AD - Marcus Aurelius dies. Commodus, son of Marcus Aurelius, becomes emporer. Northern military campign ends.
